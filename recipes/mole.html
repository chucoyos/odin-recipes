Mole

Authentic Mole

Mole is a traditional Mexican sauce that is made with a variety of ingredients. It is usually served with chicken, but can be used with other meats as well. It is a very complex sauce that can take a long time to make, but it is worth it.

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • Chicken Stock - 2 cups
  • Onion - 1 large, chopped
  • Garlic - 4 cloves, minced
  • Cinnamon - 1 stick
  • Almonds - 1 cup, toasted
  • Chocolate - 1 cup, chopped
  • Chiles - 1 cup, dried
  • Tomatoes - 1 cup, chopped
  • Oil - 1/2 cup
  • Salt - to taste

Steps

  • In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until soft.
  • Add chicken and cook until browned.
  • Add chicken stock, cinnamon, almonds, chocolate, chiles, and tomatoes. Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour.
  • Remove cinnamon stick and serve.
